# Demo Video Plan

Target length: 3 to 5 minutes.

## 1. Opening

Show the dashboard and briefly state the problem:

"Meetings contain decisions, action items, deadlines, and risks, but those details are usually buried in messy conversation. This project turns transcripts and audio into verified project execution artifacts."

## 2. Transcript Input

Paste or upload the noisy product-planning transcript.

Mention the strict extraction rules:

- Decisions require explicit confirmation.
- Actions require an explicit owner and concrete task.
- Deadlines must be explicitly stated.
- Risks are separated from questions.

## 3. Analysis Output

Click Analyze Meeting.

Show:

- Decisions count and list.
- Task cards on the execution board.
- Owners and deadlines.
- Risks panel.
- Open questions.
- CSV/JSON download buttons.

## 4. AI Copilot

Click quick prompts:

- extract
- risks
- decisions

Show that the sidebar answers from the structured output.

## 5. Audio Upload

If time allows, upload a short audio clip or mention that audio is supported through faster-whisper.

Say that the analyzer also handles unlabeled audio transcripts when owners are explicitly named.

## 6. Closing

End with:

"The key idea is verified extraction rather than generic summarization. The system avoids guessing owners, avoids inventing deadlines, and only treats confirmed statements as decisions."
